Our Mission

Koru Impact exists to serve as a catalyst for sustainable human and organizational flourishing. We build a synergistic ecosystem where rigorous social science research, evidence-based well-being practices, and purpose-driven consulting converge to empower individuals and organizations to reach their full potential and create a lasting, positive legacy.

Founder’s Biography

Şener Cem Irmak is a psychologist, systemic counsellor, and the founder of Koru Impact. He is dedicated to promoting mental health and driving organizational flourishing through evidence-based consulting and systemic empathy.

With degrees in Computer Engineering and Psychology from Bilkent University, a Master's in Neurobiology and Behavior from Universität Bielefeld, and another in Marriage and Family Therapy from Istanbul Medeniyet University, he blends scientific rigor with systemic insight.

He has worked with organizations like Zevo Health and the British International Schools of Istanbul and served in the Turkish Armed Forces as a Provisional Psychologist. Through Koru Impact, he now guides organizations in building healthier, more resilient, and purpose-driven cultures.
